About website

ESTI Canteen Online Ordering System is an art of the ICT-12 students below. This application fosters the ordering process. Students no longer have to wait in line. Also, students do not need to go down stairs just to know the foods being sold at the canteen, thus, saving them time and energy. This website also features a friendly design. The developers made the interface as simple as possible to give the users a fine experience.
